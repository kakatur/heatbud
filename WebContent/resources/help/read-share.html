Share Posts
When you find a nice post, you may want to share it with your friends.

Send by Email

You can send a Post URL by email to a list of recipients. You can include an optional personal message in the email.

*** Spamming is discouraged by Heatbud. We store your IP address and email address to report to bloggerities if needed. ***

Share on Facebook

You can share a post on Facebook with one click. You can enter an optional message that will be posted on your Facebook account. Heatbud doesn't have control on this message.

Share Elsewhere

Use the URL from the pop up window or from the address bar. Most applications extract the image and title of the post automatically.
