Guide for Bloggers

Step 1. Create an account

Login/ Signup

Step 2. Enter a Zone

Zones are topics of interest for your blog posts. You may enter an existing zone created by someone else, or create a new zone.
  • Select a zone from Now Trending Zones page.
  • Find a zone for your topic in Search page.
  • Create a Zone in Browse Zones page.

Step 3. Create a Post in this zone

To create a new blog post, click "Create a Post" under "Zone Actions".


Want to be featured in the front page of Heatbud? Contact Us after you published at least two blog posts.
