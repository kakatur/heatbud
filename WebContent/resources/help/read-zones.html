Explore Zones
Creating and managing your own blog on a traditional blogging website is very challenging. With all that effort, you will hardly see any hits on your posts.

Introducing Zones

We invented the concept of Zones where collaboration happens naturally. Zones are easy to create, require virtually no maintenance and result in ever-lasting conversations.

Enter a Zone

You will enter a zone when you click on a post title or a zone name from the Top Charts or from the Search Results.

Within a zone, the posts are sorted by the Heat Index. You can traverse forward or backward to read the posts in a zone.

Top Zones

The Top Zones section displays Heatbud zones ranked by their current popularity.

My Zones

If you are logged-in, your favorite Zones appear under My Zones.
