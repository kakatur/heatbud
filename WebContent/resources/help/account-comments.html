Comments
Comments help stir up discussion on a post.

Post a Comment

Write a productive comment and click Post Comment. Your first comment on a post increases the Heat Index of the Blogger, Post and the Zone by 3. Your subsequent comments on the same post will not alter the Heat Index.

Whenever you comment on a post, an email notification will be sent to all users who are following comments on the post.

Delete a Comment

Click the "Delete" icon next to your comment.

Follow Comments on a Post

Click Follow comments in the Comments section.

Unfollow Comments on a Post

Click Unfollow comments in the Comments section.

Unfollow Comments on all Posts

Want to unfollow all the posts that you subscribed to? Visit the Settings page.
