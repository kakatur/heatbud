Blog Post Pricing

Use this area in your profile page to let businesses know what type of blog posts you want to write, their delivery times and their pricing.

Some examples:
  • 400-500 words w/ 1 image: 5 days : $25
  • 1000 words w/ 2 images: 10 days : $35
  • 1000 words w/ 2 images: 5 days : $50 (price increased due to faster delivery)
  • 1000 words w/ a video: 10 days : $100
Images are a must in all blog posts. In general, one image is recommended for every 300-400 words. Visit Commons: Free media resources/Photography to obtain free images.

Payments:
  • When buyer closes the order, money will be sent via Paypal to the email address on your profile.
  • You must have an account with Paypal to be able to receive the money.
  • We charge 10% of the pricing as commission.
